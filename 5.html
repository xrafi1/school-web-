Welcome to Our Community of Learners

Since 2005, Sunrise Academy has been a cornerstone of education in Jamadarbari Pathar, Assam. We are committed to fostering an environment where students not only achieve academic excellence but also develop strong character and a passion for lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment with a technological orientation, which maximizes individual potential.

Our Vision

To inspire our students to be responsible global citizens who can face the future with confidence and courage.

A Path for Every Learner

Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to cater to students at every stage of their educational journey.

Primary (I-V)

Building a strong foundation with a focus on curiosity, creativity, and core concepts in a nurturing environment.

Middle (VI-VIII)

Encouraging exploration and critical thinking to help students discover their passions and strengths.

Secondary (IX-X)

Focused preparation for board exams and beyond, equipping students with knowledge and skills for success.
