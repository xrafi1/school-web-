Welcome to Sunrise Academy

Fostering a love for learning in the heart of our community since 2005.

Our Mission: To provide accessible, high-quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in a changing world. We are dedicated to creating a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment.

Our Vision: To be a leading educational institution in the region, recognized for academic excellence and for nurturing responsible, compassionate, and lifelong learners who contribute positively to society.

Located in the serene surroundings of Jamadarbari Pathar, our school provides a perfect atmosphere for learning and growth, blending modern teaching techniques with traditional values.

Admission Process for 2026-2027

  1. Pick up the admission form from the school office between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.
  2. Submit the completed form along with required documents (Birth Certificate, Previous Marksheet, Transfer Certificate, Photographs).
  3. A simple interaction session will be scheduled for the student and parents.
  4. Admission results will be communicated via phone or displayed on the school notice board.
